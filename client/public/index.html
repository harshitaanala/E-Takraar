About E-Takraar

E-Takraar is a pioneering platform dedicated to combating cybercrime and online harassment through technology-driven solutions. Our name "Takraar" signifies repetition and emphasis, highlighting our commitment to repeatedly address and emphasize the importance of cybersecurity.

Founded by a team of cybersecurity experts and data scientists, E-Takraar employs advanced machine learning algorithms to classify, analyze, and process cybercrime reports efficiently.

Our mission is to create a safer digital environment by providing an accessible platform for reporting incidents and connecting victims with the appropriate resources and authorities.
